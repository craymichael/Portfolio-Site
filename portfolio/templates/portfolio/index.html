Greetings!

Welcome to my personal site! I am currently a Ph.D. student at the Computer Vision Research Lab (CVRL) in the Computer Science & Engineering doctorate program at the University of Notre Dame.

While my research has spanned from digital accelerator design to reservoir and neuromorphic computing to deep learning applications in epidemiology, my current focus is in explainable artificial intelligence (XAI). I am actively working on opening up the deep learning black box, exploring intrinsically comprehensible algorithms, and shedding light on poor practices in interpretability research. I am passionate about responsibly deploying AI for the betterment of society, deepening our understanding of core machine learning algorithms, and contributing to the FLOSS ecosystem.

Check out my projects here and on GitHub. If you like the site and want to explore or use it yourself, view the source on GitHub.
